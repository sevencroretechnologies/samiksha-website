Product Portfolio

From Silicon to System - Our Product Lines

Semiksha develops proprietary semiconductor and embedded products that serve industrial IoT, water management, and smart infrastructure markets.

Semiconductor Division

MATOMAC Chipset Family

Our MATOMAC product line delivers application-specific semiconductor solutions for industrial and IoT applications.

MATOMAC-100

Entry-level microcontroller for basic IoT sensor nodes and data acquisition systems. Ultra-low power consumption with integrated ADC and communication peripherals.

Target: Sensor Nodes & Data Loggers

MATOMAC-200

Mid-range SoC with integrated wireless connectivity for industrial monitoring and control applications. Supports multiple communication protocols.

Target: Industrial IoT & Monitoring

MATOMAC-300

Advanced application processor with hardware AI acceleration for edge computing. Designed for machine vision and real-time analytics applications.

Target: Edge AI & Machine Vision
Embedded Products Division

Smart Embedded Solutions

Production-ready embedded products for water management, environmental monitoring, and industrial automation.

eCods

Environmental monitoring and data collection system for industrial applications. Real-time sensing, edge processing, and cloud connectivity for environmental compliance.

Jalvidha Series

Smart water level monitoring and management system. Available in multiple variants for residential, commercial, and municipal water infrastructure applications.

Jal Mudra

Water quality monitoring device with multi-parameter sensing. Measures pH, TDS, turbidity, and dissolved oxygen for water treatment plants and distribution networks.

Jalni-RO Controller

Intelligent controller for reverse osmosis water purification systems. Automated monitoring, filter life tracking, and smart alerts for maintenance scheduling.
