About Semiksha

Built by Engineers. Driven by Innovation.

Semiksha is a deep technology company specializing in semiconductor chip design and embedded product development. Founded by a team of seasoned engineers, we solve problems that require expertise down to the transistor level.

To accelerate hardware innovation by making world-class semiconductor and embedded engineering accessible to every product team.

To be one of the leading technology fabless chip development and embedded engineering organizations in Machine Type Communication, Mechatronics, and Automotive industries.

We are committed to promoting the Make in India initiative, building semiconductor capabilities in Tier II cities with a strong conviction, clear vision, and determination to lead the Indian semiconductor revolution.
