What We Do

End-to-End Semiconductor & Embedded Engineering

Semiconductor / ASIC Design

Custom silicon built for your application. We provide end-to-end ASIC design services including RTL design & verification, FPGA prototyping, logic synthesis, timing closure, physical design, and tape-out support. Our team has experience with multiple process nodes and industry-standard EDA tools.

  • RTL Design & Verification (Verilog/VHDL)
  • FPGA Prototyping & Validation
  • Logic Synthesis & Timing Closure
  • Physical Design & DFT
  • Tape-out Support

Embedded Systems Development

From bare metal to real-time OS environments. We develop robust embedded software solutions including firmware development, board support packages, RTOS integration, device drivers, and communication protocol stacks for industrial and consumer applications.

  • Firmware Development (C/C++)
  • Board Support Package (BSP)
  • RTOS Integration (FreeRTOS, Zephyr)
  • Device Driver Development
  • Communication Protocols (UART, SPI, I2C, CAN)

Hardware / PCB Design

Precision hardware engineering from concept to production-ready boards. We deliver schematic capture, multi-layer PCB layout, signal integrity analysis, power integrity analysis, thermal management, and DFM reviews for complex mixed-signal designs.

  • Schematic Capture & Review
  • Multi-layer PCB Layout (up to 16+ layers)
  • Signal & Power Integrity Analysis
  • Thermal Management
  • DFM/DFA Reviews

AI at the Edge

Deploy machine learning models on resource-constrained edge hardware. We specialize in model optimization, quantization, NPU/GPU integration, and real-time inference pipelines for computer vision, predictive maintenance, and anomaly detection applications.

  • Model Optimization & Quantization
  • NPU/GPU Integration
  • Real-time Inference Pipelines
  • Computer Vision at Edge
  • Predictive Maintenance Models

Product Engineering & Consulting

From idea to market-ready product. We provide end-to-end product development consulting including feasibility studies, architecture design, technology selection, prototype development, certification support, and manufacturing liaison.

  • Feasibility Studies & Architecture Design
  • Technology Selection & Roadmapping
  • Prototype Development
  • Certification Support (CE, FCC, IEC)
  • Manufacturing Liaison

Industries We Serve

Our deep-tech expertise serves clients across a range of industries. We understand the unique requirements, compliance standards, and design constraints of each sector.

  • Industrial IoT & Smart Manufacturing
  • Automotive & EV Systems
  • Consumer Electronics
  • Defense & Aerospace
  • Medical Devices (IEC 60601-1)
