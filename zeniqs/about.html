About Us

Who We Are

Built by Engineers. Driven by Innovation.

Semiksha is a deep technology company specializing in semiconductor chip design and embedded product development. Established in 2018, we were founded by Vijay Gudi and a team of seasoned engineers with a vision to accelerate hardware innovation in India.

We solve problems that require expertise down to the transistor level - from custom ASIC design to embedded firmware, from PCB layout to AI at the edge. Our engineering team brings decades of combined experience across VLSI, embedded systems, and hardware design.

Based at KLE CTIE in Hubballi, Karnataka, we are proud to be part of the Make in India semiconductor initiative, building world-class capabilities in a Tier II city.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Goals

Our Mission

To accelerate hardware innovation by making world-class semiconductor and embedded engineering accessible to every product team, anywhere in the world.

Our Vision

To be one of the leading technology fabless chip development and embedded engineering organizations in Machine Type Communication, Mechatronics, and Automotive industries.

Our Goal

To innovate and develop a range of products and technologies that address real-world challenges and contribute to India's semiconductor revolution from Tier II cities.
