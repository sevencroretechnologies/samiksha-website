The People Behind Semiksha

Our Core Team

A team of passionate engineers with decades of combined experience in semiconductor design, embedded systems, and hardware engineering.

Vijay Gudi

Founder & CEO

Passionate about building innovative embedded solutions, Vijay founded Semiksha with a vision to create world-class semiconductor capabilities in India. Under his leadership, the company has successfully launched 4 embedded products and established partnerships with leading technology firms. He drives the company's strategic vision and product roadmap.

Pranesh Kulkarni

Hardware Design Lead

With 15+ years of experience in hardware design, Pranesh specializes in Medical and Consumer Electronics product development. His expertise spans IEC 60601-1 compliance, CE marking, and complex multi-layer PCB designs. He leads all hardware engineering initiatives at Semiksha.

Girish Hulamani

Embedded Systems Lead

Girish brings 17+ years of deep expertise in Embedded Linux, C programming, and device driver development. He has architected firmware for industrial controllers, IoT gateways, and consumer devices. His proficiency in real-time systems and communication protocols is at the core of Semiksha's embedded offerings.

Dr. Ramakrishna S Joshi

Wireless & AI Advisor

Dr. Joshi holds a Ph.D. in Wireless Communication and brings 15 years of research and industry experience in NB-LTE, Machine Vision, and Machine Learning. He advises Semiksha on advanced wireless protocols, AI integration strategies, and next-generation communication technologies.
